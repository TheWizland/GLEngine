Skybox images are provided as six files, named as such:
xp.png (Right)
xn.png (Left)
yp.png (Top)
yn.png (Bottom)
zp.png (Front)
zn.png (Back)
Extension does not have to be .png, may be any that stb_image.h can handle.
Path to the folder and extension are provided as parameters to SkyboxRenderer::init()
